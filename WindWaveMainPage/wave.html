The great majority of large breakers seen at a beach result from distant winds.

Five factors influence the formation of the flow structures in wind waves:

1. Wind speed or strength relative to wave speed;

2. The wind blows without significant change in direction;

3. Width of the area affected by fetch;

4. The time for which the wind has blown over the water;

5. Water depth.
